Experience Hegra

The ancient Nabataean city of Hegra is located in the northwest of the Arabian Peninsula, in the flagship tourist destination of AlUla, Saudi Arabia. While its more well-known sister city of Petra lies in Jordan, the 110 tombs of Hegra are still waiting to truly be discovered.

AlUla, Saudi Arabia

Explore the history of AlUla

Situated at the heart of the ancient Incense Road, AlUla was once the southernmost outpost of the great Nabataean civilization. There are many ways to explore the over 110 tombs of Saudi Arabia's first UNESCO World Heritage Site, though all options involve booking a tour through AlUla's destination website. From sunrise horse-drawn carriage rides to after-dark experiences, each option gives a unique glimpse into this historic city.

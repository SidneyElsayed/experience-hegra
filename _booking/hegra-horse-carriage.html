What's Included

✓ Free parking at the Hegra Visitor Center

✓ Welcome and hospitality (seasonal drinks, dates, sweets) at the Visitor Center

✓ Transportation within Hegra by horse-drawn carriage

✓ Entrance to various designated heritage sites within Hegra

✓ English & Arabic speaking Rawis (storytellers) and Rangers (heritage support) on-site to offer historical context and guidance

✕ Transportation to and from the Hegra Visitor Center

✕ Entry inside tombs; only exterior viewing is permitted during the tour

Pro Tip: Book the entire carriage for a more private experience. Each carriage fits up to 4 people.

Meeting Location

Hegra South Gate

For this more intimate tour, you can drive directly to Hegra. Make sure to use the Hegra Visitor Center Google pin, as others won't take you directly to the entrance. Free parking is available behind the Visitor Center.

View Google pin for the Hegra Visitor Center

What to Expect

Experience the spectacular views of Hegra at sunrise or sunset from atop a horse-drawn carriage. A designated horseman will take you through the area of Hegra, bringing you to various sites. Your Rawi, or storyteller, will explain different details of the tombs, carvings and more. Each guide is accompanied by a Ranger, who is there to ensure visitors respect the heritage sites.

The tour generally includes the following:

  • Jabal Ahmar
  • Tomb of Lihyan son of Kuza
  • Jabal Banat

*Some sites may be under renovation/restoration at the time of your visit - parts of Hegra are still being discovered, which is what makes it an exciting time to visit.

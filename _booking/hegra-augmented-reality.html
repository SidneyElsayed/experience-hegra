What to Expect

This augmented reality tour brings the ancient Nabataean civilization to life with special ARtGlass technologies. Climb aboard your own private vintage Land Rover and see a side of Hegra not many have seen before.

A designated driver will take you through the area of Hegra, bringing you to various sites. Most of the tour guides, known as Rawis (singular: raw-wee), are from AlUla or the surrounding area. Each guide is accompanied by a Ranger, who is there to ensure visitors respect the heritage sites. Your Rawi will explain different details of the tombs, carvings and more. The tour generally includes the following:

  • Jabal Ahmar
  • Tomb of Lihyan son of Kuza
  • Jabal Banat
  • Jabal Ithlib
  • Handcraft Pavilion

*Some sites may be under renovation/restoration at the time of your visit - parts of Hegra are still being discovered, which is what makes it an exciting time to visit.

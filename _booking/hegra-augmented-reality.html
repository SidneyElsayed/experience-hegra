What's Included

✓ Free parking at the Hegra Visitor Center

✓ Welcome and hospitality (seasonal drinks, dates, sweets) at the Visitor Center

✓ Augmented reality glasses which use ARtGlass technologies.

✓ Access to special 'no-go areas'

✓ Private transportation within Hegra by vintage Land Rover

✓ Entrance to various designated heritage sites within Hegra

✓ English & Arabic speaking Rawis (storytellers) and Rangers (heritage support) on-site to offer historical context and guidance

✕ Transportation to and from the Hegra Visitor Center

✕ Entry inside tombs; only exterior viewing is permitted during the tour

Meeting Location

Hegra South Gate

For this more intimate tour, you can drive directly to Hegra. Make sure to use the Hegra Visitor Center Google pin, as others won't take you directly to the entrance. Free parking is available behind the Visitor Center.

What to Expect

This augmented reality tour brings the ancient Nabataean civilization to life with special ARtGlass technologies. Climb aboard your own private vintage Land Rover and see a side of Hegra not many have seen before.

A designated driver will take you through the area of Hegra, bringing you to various sites. Most of the tour guides, known as Rawis (singular: raw-wee), are from AlUla or the surrounding area. Each guide is accompanied by a Ranger, who is there to ensure visitors respect the heritage sites. Your Rawi will explain different details of the tombs, carvings and more. The tour generally includes the following:

  • Jabal Ahmar
  • Tomb of Lihyan son of Kuza
  • Jabal Banat
  • Jabal Ithlib
  • Handcraft Pavilion

*Some sites may be under renovation/restoration at the time of your visit - parts of Hegra are still being discovered, which is what makes it an exciting time to visit.

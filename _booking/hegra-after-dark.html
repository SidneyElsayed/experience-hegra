Meeting Location

Hegra South Gate

If you'd like, you can drive directly to Hegra for the start of the tour. Make sure to use the Hegra South Gate Google pin, as others won't take you directly to the entrance. Free parking is available behind the Visitor Center.

Winter Park

You can also park for free at Winter Park and take a 30 minute coach bus transfer. Make sure to check your ticket for what time to catch the coach bus from Winter Park to Hegra.

What to Expect

Saudi is well known for its hospitality, which they are excited to share. From the moment you step into the Visitor Center, you'll be welcomed with an array of drinks and treats. Depending on the season, this could be fresh juices or Saudi coffee, water, dried fruits and always a few dates. Most of the tour guides, known as Rawis (singular: raw-wee), are from AlUla or the surrounding area. Each guide is accompanied by a Ranger, who is there to ensure visitors respect the heritage sites.

The coach will drive through the area of Hegra, bringing you to various designated sites. Your Rawi will explain different details of the tombs, carvings and more. The tour generally includes the following:

  • Jabal Ahmar
  • Tomb of Lihyan son of Kuza
  • Jabal Banat
  • Jabal Ithlib
  • Handcraft Pavilion

*Some sites may be under renovation/restoration at the time of your visit - parts of Hegra are still being discovered, which is what makes it an exciting time to visit.
